### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We're using Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and store certain info. This tracking tech includes cool stuff like beacons, tags, and scripts. They're all about collecting and tracking info, and helping us make our Service even better. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies:** A Cookie is just a tiny file chillin' on your device. You can tell your browser to kick all Cookies to the curb or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But hey, if you don't take Cookies, you might miss out on some parts of our Service. Unless you've set your browser to reject 'em, our Service might just use Cookies anyway.
- **Web Beacons:** Some parts of our Service and our emails might have these tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs). These guys let the Company (for example) count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and help with other website stats (like checking out how popular a section is and verifying system and server integrity).

**Cookies** come in two flavors: "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even after you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We totally use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies:**
  - **Type:** Session Cookies
  - **Managed by:** Us
  - **Purpose:** These Cookies are super crucial for hooking you up with services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They're a big help in authenticating users and making sure no funny business (like fraudulent use of user accounts) is going down. Without these Cookies, the services you're asking for just can't be delivered, and we only use these Cookies to give you those specific services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies:**
  - **Type:** Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by:** Us
  - **Purpose:** These Cookies are used to check if users have given the thumbs-up to the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies:**
  - **Type:** Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by:** Us
  - **Purpose:** These Cookies are neat because they let us remember choices you make when you're using the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you visit the Website.

For more info on the Cookies we use and your options regarding them, just swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
